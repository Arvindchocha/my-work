About us

WWW.PHILOMENAKLOSS.COM

We are Nadia and Tanya. and we made project Philomena Kloss in 2010. We just enjoyed doing what we liked - embroidering, knitting, making home textiles and interior toys. The creative imagination has no limits, but we have always dreamed of something really independent and inimitable. At last one lucky day the image of the first Philomena Kloss bear appeared. The toy turned out to be both funny and serious, just the way we would like it to be.

Now we are sending our bears to 4 continents, they have found their homes all over the world from the USA to New Zealand! We're so happy to know that our work gives you pleasure and makes you smile. We're happy, when you and your bear become friends

Thank you!
